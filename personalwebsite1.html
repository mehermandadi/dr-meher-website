About Me

With over 25 years of experience, I have dedicated my career to teaching and guiding students in various subjects including C, C++, Python, Java, Web Technologies, R, SQL, Tableau, PowerBI, ChatGPT, and other AI tools. I have received several awards including the Best Principal Award by TASK IT Ministry of Telangana in 2017, Best Teacher, and Best Resource Person in 2008 & 2009. I have supervised 5 Ph.D. students, with 2 awarded Ph.D.'s and 2 who have submitted their theses and are awaiting final viva. I have held positions as Professor CSE, HOD IT & CSE, Principal of an engineering college, Director of R&D, and Director of Student Affairs. Many of my students work in reputable national and international companies like Google, Microsoft, Amazon, TCS, and as entrepreneurs in India, USA, UK, Canada, Gulf countries, etc.

I am also the founder of Meher Software Solutions, co-founder of MMP Rao Software Solutions LLP, and founder of Mehera Meher Group.
